RedBrick Hacks 2021 is Ashoka University’s flagship 36-hour Hackathon. With rapid advancements in technology over the past decade, there is an unprecedented need to design for the future by anticipating the obstacles in the present. We can’t wait to witness the zeal and innovation with which you do it! To aid you in this process, we have organized a two-day Hacker’s Workshop a week prior to the Hackathon. This will help to brush up your skills and get back in your coding mode.
